Welcome To

Freestyle Dojo


We are located in Toronto, Ontario serving the Greater Toronto Area! The name Freestyle is due to how we teach and encourage our students to make martial arts their own. We teach only the highest level of technique focusing on strong understanding of concepts and creativity in execution. Everyone is welcome at Freestyle Dojo, we offer both a friendly learning environment for beginners and hard competitive training sessions for advanced martial artists. About This School

About Us

Freestyle Dojo specializes in No-gi Jiu-Jitsu and MMA style striking. We work to uplift every student past their limits on and off the mat. We strive to build strong communities and camaraderie primarily through training but also This comes through social events like holiday celebrations, hangouts, and UFC watch parties! What sets us apart from other gyms is the breadth of technique and holistic approach we take to training. From their first month our students are taught with the ultimate goal in mind, to create as complete a martial artist as possible. All our lessons are taught with safety and self-care in mind, instructing students how to protect themselves and their training partners. We ensure the best instruction with a variety of methods: coaches and students rolling together, 1-on-1 time, after-class help, and tape review. All of this awaits you at Freestyle Dojo!

Meet the Coaches Behind

Freestyle Dojo

Mike Romano
Head Coach/Owner

Head coach, owner and founder of Freestyle Dojo. Mike's laid back demeanor balanced with his love for hard work really sets the tone of Freestyle. He believes Jiu Jitsu can be the vehicle for self growth, challenge and community that everyone is looking for. After 17 years of Nogi training at the top gyms in Canada, Mike's classes offer a great balance of traditional drills, CLA and live sparring to help you reach your maximum potential.

Jesse Bell
Morning Coach/Owner

Coach, owner and co-founder of Freestyle Dojo. Jesse's coaching style is rooted in precision and technical excellence. With a broad and deep knowledge of all things Nogi, his teaching will leave you with a complete understanding of the class curriculum. With boundless creativity and enthusiasm for grappling, Jesse can alter you're very perception of what is possible. To sum him up in 3 words, Precision, excellence, creativity.

Daniel Justino
Coach

A black belt from Toronto Nogi, Daniel lends his skills and expertise every Monday for Fundamentals. He teaches a classic style that is a perfect introduction for our beginners. With absolutely no wasted movement or unnecessary explanation, Daniel knows how to optimize you're learning. In the truest spirit of Jiu Jitsu the power of skill and leverage.

Max Ullman
Kids Coach

Max brings an unmathed energy to our kids program. With a background in wrestling and a degree in childhood education, you can rest assured you're kids are under great guidance. Max is tough, thoughtful, patient and tenacious. He knows how to lead a room and bring out the best in his students.

Graham Dobs
Strength & Conditioning Coach

One of our first students, Graham excels in everything he does. From rugby to Jiu Jitsu and now coaching strength and conditioning. He combines all of his experience into making you better, faster and stronger.

Programs

At Freestyle Dojo, we offer a variety of programs designed to help students of all ages and experience levels develop real-world self-defense skills in a supportive, high-energy environment. Whether you’re looking for structured learning, a fun and engaging class for your child, or an immersive training experience, we have the perfect program for you. Our classes emphasize skill development, personal growth, and community, ensuring that every student finds their place on the mats.

NOGI / Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Grappling Without Limits

Our BJJ program is built on freedom, adaptability, and high-level technique. Specializing in No Gi Jiu Jitsu with a strong focus on wrestling, submissions, and real-life application, our classes cater to beginners and experienced grapplers alike. We break down complex techniques, teach smart control, and prioritize safety to help you become a well-rounded, confident grappler. Whether you’re here for self-defense, competition, or just to sharpen your skills, Freestyle Dojo will take your Jiu Jitsu to the next level.

  • Adults No Gi BJJ (16+)

  • Kids (Ages 5-8)

  • Kids (Ages 9-11)

  • Teens (Ages 12-15)

Striking Program

Brothers Mike Romano and Jesse Bell opened our doors at Freestyle Dojo in 2021. The two wanted to give a space to people who don’t want the rigid structure that martial arts could provide. They always wanted to just show up, respect who they trained with, and get to work without anyone feeling above anybody else. Mike and Jesse have trained across North America and worked with different coaches and many students, including training in the 10th Planet Jiu Jitsu System. This inspired their grappling focus and teaching locks that may not be competition-oriented but is the most freeing and self-defense focused. Freedom in training and becoming emphatic is the main focus of Freestyle Dojo.
