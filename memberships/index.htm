Find the Right Membership for You

Choosing the right membership is the first step in your Jiu Jitsu journey. At Freestyle Dojo, we offer flexible plans to fit your lifestyle—whether you’re training for self-defense, competition, or personal growth. With expert coaching, a supportive community, and a focus on real-world grappling, our memberships give you access to top-tier instruction in a laid-back, hard-working environment. Find the plan that works for you and start training today!

Perfect for those with a busy schedule or looking to supplement their training, our 1 Class/Week membership allows you to stay sharp, build skills, and train in our welcoming community. Get high-quality instruction while balancing your lifestyle.

Take your Jiu Jitsu to the next level with 2 Classes/Week, giving you the perfect blend of consistency and flexibility. Improve your grappling, submissions, and situational awareness with structured, high-level instruction.

For those who want full immersion, our Unlimited Membership grants you access to as many classes as you want each month. Train freely, refine your skills, and be part of our thriving community. Your Jiu Jitsu journey is limitless at Freestyle Dojo!
